ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Dominic and I LOVE web development.

How did I get to where I am today?
Well it all started some years ago after graduating from HTL Salzburg. I was ready to change the world!
...but felt like I still lacked the skills needed to do so. After about a year of teaching myself the basics of web development whenever I had free time I was ready for the next step.
So I joined the amazing guys at Salzburg Digital. I learned a lot there and this time will always have a special place in my heart. But it was time to move on and reach even higher peaks. If you are like me and believe that creativity and a healthy bit of chaos can make the world a better place (or at least a more interesting one) I would love to hear from you!
